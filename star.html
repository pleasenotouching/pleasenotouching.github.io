You're a Star


girlsRemix_final from KK on Vimeo.



[You're a Star] is a remix of found Youtube footage that reveals through video editing techniques the latent concerns behind young people's innocent search for social media stardom. The concept from the artist's anxieties and discomfort with the ubiquity of social media, and the unprecedented bilateral accessibility introduced by tit. The video is password protected out of respect for the original video uploaders (password: newmedia).
