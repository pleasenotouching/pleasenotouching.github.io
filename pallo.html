My Pal Pallo



Pallo is a curious ゆるキャラ (yuru-chara) that inhabits Instagram. Pallo is born from the Japanese tradition of yuru-chara, which are cute mascot characters created and associated with specific areas, businesses or organizations. Pallo's creepy-cute character is also an homage to the kimo-kawaii ("gross-cute," キモかわいい) branch of Asian aesthetics of cuteness. People who follow Pallo will be followed back. Pallo wants to be your friend.
